This app helps you calculate and visualize relative frequencies and other basic statistical measures from your data.

Frequency Distribution

Frequency distribution helps us organize raw data into groups or intervals, showing how often each value or range appears. For each group, the frequency tells us the count of data points, cumulative frequency shows the running total up to (or above) that group, and relative frequency (percentage) expresses the frequency as a proportion of the total number of data points. These parameters make it easy to analyze patterns and trends within a dataset.
